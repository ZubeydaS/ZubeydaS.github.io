About

Let me introduce myself.

Hi, I'm Zubeyda, a third-year Computer Science major at Carleton College and also pursuing a minor in Philosophy. Originally from Maine, I'm passionate about how technology intersects with society, especially when it comes to ethical considerations. My interest in ethics and technology is strongly tied to the rapid evolution of new technologies and the need to bridge the gap between human understanding and technological progress. I believe in the importance of ensuring that as technology evolves, it does so in a way that protects human interests while fostering innovation. I aim to contribute to conversations about responsible innovation in artificial intelligence, privacy, and automation. In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my two cats, Darien and Mateo, as well as baking and reading. As a first-generation student, I'm driven by a desire to make an impact in the tech world and create opportunities for others. I'm currently seeking career opportunities where I can further explore software engineering and/or ethical tech for the Summer of 2025. So If you’re interested in discussing anything from technology to ethics or if you just want to chat about cats or baking, feel free to reach out. I'm excited about the future, and I can't wait to see where my path in tech leads me. Thank you for stopping by!
